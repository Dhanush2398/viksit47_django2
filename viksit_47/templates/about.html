About Us

Welcome to Viksit47

Viksit47 Academy is a knowledge hub dedicated to shaping the future of students and young aspirants through quality education and holistic guidance.

Agriculture Quota Exam Guidance – We specialize in providing complete support for Agriculture Quota practical exams. Our programs include standard study materials, structured mock tests, and offline classes with live specimen demonstrations to ensure students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure.

Youth Empowerment through Education – At our core, we believe education is the strongest tool for empowerment. Viksit47 Academy is committed to inspiring and equipping today’s youth to become tomorrow’s leaders.

Vision for Viksit Bharat 2047 – The Academy is aligned with the national vision of India’s growth story. By nurturing talent and instilling confidence, we contribute towards building a self-reliant and developed India by 2047.

Competitive Exam Coaching – Alongside agriculture quota preparation, we also offer focused coaching for prestigious examinations such as ICAR-JRF, CUET, and other competitive tests, helping students pursue higher studies and career opportunities in agriculture and beyond.

Viksit47 Academy is not just an institute – it is a movement to create educated, skilled, and empowered citizens for a stronger Bharat.

About the Founder

Tejas H. N, an agriculture student from GKVK, Bangalore, founded Viksit47 Academy with a vision to empower youths through education and contribute to the dream of Viksit Bharat 2047. Coming from an agricultural background, he understands how students struggle in preparing for exams and is determined to guide them with the right support and inspiration.

He shared his vision on youth empowerment in the Viksit Bharat Young Leaders Dialogue at Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi, a historic program organized under the leadership of Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Tejas has also represented youth voices in the Youth Parliament, delivered motivational speeches across districts, and actively worked in farmers’ welfare initiatives and student guidance programs, bridging education with grassroots impact.

Through Viksit47 Academy, he strives to build a movement of skilled, confident, and socially responsible youth who will lead India into Viksit Bharat 2047.
